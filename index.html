Quarantined Community

| Keeping us all productive during Isolation

Coronavirus is undoubtedbly ripping apart families, businesses and general community, through it's seemingly unstoppable contagion. So it's time to come together. Let's not feel isolated during isolation. Let's quarantine together and rebuild our sense of community.

Keep Productive

Mind

It's v v v important to keep your mind busy and proactive, whether thats WFH, learning new skills and touching base with yourself through meditating. The outcome of all of these = retained sanity!

Body

It's v v v important to keep your body busy and proactive, whether thats eating healthily to boost your immune system, or, keeping your body moving through regular exercise.

How can I help?

Although its important to keep ourselves entertained, its also important to ensure the safety of ourselves and others. Here you will find what you can do to stay safe, and what you can do to help others.

MIND
Keep it active!

A number of hobbies you can try out: 1. Cooking / Baking 2. Learning a new language 3. Read books 4. Practising a new skill to help with you career

Keep it healthy!

A number of activities to try out: 1. Keep a journal 2. Practise meditation / mindfulness 3. Download apps such as Headspace or Calm

BODY
Keep it active!

A number of exercises to try out at home: 1. home workout videos 2. yoga 3. HIIT workouts 4. hula hooping

Keep it healthy!

A number of immuno-boosting foods to involve in your diet: 1. Citrus fruits 2. Fiber-rich vegetables 3. Yoghurt - vitamin D regulates immune system 4. Green Tea - antioxidative compounds 5. Shellfish - Zinc, encourages normal function of our immune cells

HOW CAN I HELP
Helping ourselves

A number of precautionary measures to help prevent us from catching COVID-19: 1. Wear a facemask that covers your nose and mouth 2. Thouroughly wash your hands for 20 seconds everytime after returning home 3. Sneeze into your elbow 4. Adhere to social distancing 5. Stay at home as much as possible

Help others

A number of precautionary measures to help prevent spread COVID-19, and help those in need: 1. Adhere to social distancing 2. Stay at home as much as possible 3. If you have the required skills of a fronline worker, find out how you can be of help / volunteer.

Think you can help? Our contact page provides some useful links for you to explore some opporutnities for you to offer your skills and support.
