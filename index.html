Quarantined Community

| Keeping us all productive during Isolation

Coronavirus is undoubtedbly effecting families, businesses and general community, through it's seemingly unstoppable contagion. So it's time to come together. Let's not feel isolated during isolation. Let's quarantine together and rebuild our sense of community.

Mind

It's important to keep your mind busy and proactive, whether thats through WFH, learning new skills or touching base with yourself every now and again. During these difficult times it is easy to become uneasy, so the aim of these recommeneded practices is to encourage a sense of maintained sanity

Body

Your body is likely having to adapt to a changed lifestyle during quarantine, so it's important to treat it as best we can. Whether thats eating healthily to boost your immune system, or, keeping your body moving through regular exercise

How can I help?

Although we need to keep ourselves entertained, its also important to ensure the safety of ourselves as well as others. Here you will find what you can do to stay safe, and what you can do to help others

MIND
Keep it active

A number of hobbies you can try out:

  1. Cooking / Baking
  2. Learning a new language
  3. Reading books
  4. Practicing a new skill to help with you career
Keep it healthy

A number of activities to try out:

  1. Keep a journal
  2. Practise meditation / mindfulness
  3. Download apps such as Headspace or Calm
  4. Don't obsess over the news
BODY
Keep it active

A number of exercises to try out at home:

  1. Home workout videos
  2. Yoga
  3. HIIT workouts
  4. Hula hooping
  5. Weight trainings

Keep it healthy

A number of immuno-boosting foods to involve in your diet:

  1. Citrus fruits
  2. Fiber-rich vegetables
  3. Yoghurt - vitamin D regulates immune system
  4. Green Tea - antioxidative compounds
  5. Shellfish - Zinc, encourages normal function of our immune cells

HOW CAN I HELP?
Helping ourselves

A number of precautionary measures to help prevent us from catching COVID-19:

  1. Wear a facemask that covers your nose and mouth
  2. Thouroughly wash your hands for 20 seconds everytime after returning home
  3. Sneeze into your elbow
  4. Adhere to social distancing
  5. Stay at home as much as possible

Helping others

A number of precautionary measures to help prevent spread COVID-19, and help those in need:

  1. Adhere to social distancing
  2. Stay at home as much as possible
  3. Volunteer to speak on the phone with those who feel lonley
  4. Raise funds for charities / hospitals who are in need
  5. If you have the required skills of a frontline worker, find out how you can be of help / volunteer.

Think you can help? Our contact page provides some useful links to explore some opporutnities for you to offer your skills and support.
